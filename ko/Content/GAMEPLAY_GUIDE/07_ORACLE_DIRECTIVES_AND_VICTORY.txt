07) OBJECTIVES, DIRECTIVES, AND VICTORY
---------------------------------------

VICTORY: THE CURRENT RULE
-------------------------
The campaign has two normal win paths. Whichever resolves first wins:

1. Control `(0,0)` and raise the culture-specific Wormhole to level `100`.
2. Control `5` Keystone colonies in the same faction, each with at least
   `1,000` Visions.

Surrender can still end a multiplayer campaign early. If every other campaign
faction surrenders, the last unsurrendered faction wins immediately.

THE ORIGIN WORMHOLE
-------------------
The `(0,0)` tile is the Origin Wormhole site. It begins under a static KRAKEN
Void garrison. Clear the garrison before colonising it.

Once colonised, the site can build only:
- the Wormhole structure
- Silo
- Solar Cell
- Ion Shield up to level `20`

The Wormhole is culture-specific:
- Veil: `Sanctum of Vael`
- Astraean: `Lens of Aster`
- Varkon: `Furnace of Vorrak`

The Wormhole cannot exceed the lower of Silo and Solar Cell. To build
Wormhole `30`, both storage structures must be at least `30`.

KEYSTONE VISION VICTORY
-----------------------
Each quadrant contains an Inner Keystone and an Outer Keystone. These sites
also begin under static KRAKEN garrisons.

Once colonised, a Keystone can build only:
- Keystone Vision
- Silo
- Solar Cell
- Ion Shield up to level `20`

Keystone Vision cannot exceed the lower of Silo and Solar Cell. The structure
converts donated Noctmarks into Keystone Visions over time.

Keystone structure names depend on culture and tier:
- Veil Inner: `Reliquary of Vael`
- Veil Outer: `Obelisk of Vael`
- Astraean Inner: `Prism of Aster`
- Astraean Outer: `Orrery of Aster`
- Varkon Inner: `Anvil of Vorrak`
- Varkon Outer: `Crucible of Vorrak`

NOCTMARK DONATION AND CONVERSION
--------------------------------
Faction members can donate Noctmarks to Keystone colonies controlled by their
faction. Donations can come from personal funds or from organizations the
player controls.

The conversion rate improves with Keystone Vision level:

`NoctmarksPerVision = 100 / 100 ^ ((level - 1) / 99)`

That means:
- level `1`: `100` Noctmarks per Vision
- level `100`: `1` Noctmark per Vision

Conversion is passive and capped per hour by Keystone Vision level. Higher
levels convert faster and waste fewer Noctmarks.

OBJECTIVE INTEL
---------------
Objective sites can be attacked and spied on, but they cannot be raided.

Enemy Wormhole and Keystone progress is not live public information. Victory
and faction objective screens show enemy objective values only from the last
successful spy snapshot. Faction-owned objective colonies show live values to
their faction.

DIRECTIVES: HOW THE SYSTEM REALLY WORKS
---------------------------------------
Directives are long-form progression paths.

Core rules:
- `12` paths
- `10` stages each
- all paths run in parallel
- stages auto-complete when their conditions are met
- there is no claim button
- finishing a full path unlocks a permanent buff

DIRECTIVE PATHS AND PERMANENT BUFFS
-----------------------------------
- `INTEL / Signal Cartographer`: `+12%` spy survival
- `RAIDER / Void Reaver`: `+10%` raid loot
- `FIGHTER / Aegis Vanguard`: `+8%` fleet attack
- `ECON / Solar Ledger`: `+6%` base production
- `DIPLO / Concord Envoy`: `+20%` diplomacy penalty resistance
- `TRADER / Starlane Broker`: `+8%` fleet cargo
- `BIZ / Consortium Founder`: `+8%` base storage
- `EXPAND / Frontier Ascendant`: `+8%` travel speed
- `INDUSTRY / Forge Architect`: `+8%` build speed and `+8%` ship-build speed
- `DEFENSE / Bastion Warden`: `+10%` base defense and `+6%` fleet defense
- `LOGI / Flux Quartermaster`: `+10%` travel speed and `+6%` fleet cargo
- `AUGMENT / Noctium Artificer`: `+8%` research speed and `+10%`
  transmute speed

HOW TO THINK ABOUT DIRECTIVES
-----------------------------
Directives are not side quests. They are a second layer of empire shape.

Examples:
- a raider empire that finishes `RAIDER` and `LOGI` becomes much better at
  repeated economic pressure
- a Keystone faction that finishes `ECON`, `INDUSTRY`, and `AUGMENT` scales
  production, construction, and conversion support all at once
- a defensive Origin holder loves `DEFENSE`, `INDUSTRY`, and `EXPAND`

PRACTICAL DIRECTIVE ADVICE
--------------------------
1. Because all paths run in parallel, broad play naturally progresses more
   directives than narrow tunnel-vision play.

2. Spy, trade, raid, expansion, and production all have long-term value even if
   they are not your main win condition.

3. If you ignore a whole layer of the game, you also ignore the directive buff
   attached to it.

THE WINNING MINDSET
-------------------
Late-game planning should stay centered on this question:
"Can my faction keep one objective path moving while protecting it from spies,
siege, and coordinated counterattacks?"
