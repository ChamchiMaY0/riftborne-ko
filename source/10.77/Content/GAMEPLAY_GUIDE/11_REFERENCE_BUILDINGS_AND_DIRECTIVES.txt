11) REFERENCE: BUILDINGS AND DIRECTIVES
---------------------------------------

BUILDINGS
---------

RESOURCE AND STORAGE
- Extractor (max 20): increases Vulkron output by the campaign's normal
  per-level resource bonus
- Synthesiser (max 20): increases Aurelite output by the campaign's normal
  per-level resource bonus
- Combinator (max 20): increases Deuterium output by the campaign's normal
  per-level resource bonus
- Solar Array (max 20): increases Astra output by the campaign's normal
  per-level resource bonus
- Dyson Sphere (max 200): uses the same per-level Astra bonus, but with a much
  higher cap as the late-game sink
- Silo (max 20): storage for Vulkron, Aurelite, and Deuterium
- Solar Cell (max 20): storage for Astra

COMMAND AND TEMPO
- Central Spire (max 25): +10% build speed per level; L5, L10, L15, and
  L21-L25 each unlock one generic outpost slot from that origin, and L20
  unlocks one new-colony slot
- Light Shipyard (max 20): +10% light/intelligence build and travel speed per level, plus +2% light-hull stats per level
- Heavy Shipyard (max 20): +10% heavy/cargo/colonization build and travel speed per level, plus +2% heavy-hull stats per level
- Siege Foundry (max 20): +10% bomber/siege build and travel speed per level, plus +2% siege-hull stats per level; bombers unlock at L5 and siege hulls at L15
- Orbital Exchange (max 20): +1% launched-fleet cargo per level and +1% logistics shipment speed per level
- Flagship Research (max 5): +15% research speed and +3% build speed per
  level
- Objective structures:
  - Origin Wormhole at `(0,0)` (max 100), gated by Silo and Solar Cell
  - Keystone Vision on Keystone colonies (max 100), converts donated Noctmarks
    into Keystone Visions over time

DEFENSE AND SUSTAIN
- Ion Shield (max 20): culture-scaled shield strength
  - Astraean: +7.5% defense and +75 flat defense per level (2.5x at L20)
  - Varkon: +1% defense and +10 flat defense per level (1.2x at L20)
  - Veil: +5% defense and +50 flat defense per level (2.0x at L20)
- Nano Storage (max 20): `cargoPenalty = min(0.80, 0.04 * level)`
- Drone Facility (max 20): `upkeepReduction = min(0.80, 0.04 * level)`

CULTURE BUILDINGS
- Overclocker (Astraean, max 20): boosts all four resource outputs. In any
  save, each level gives half of that save's normal resource-building bonus.
- Conscription Service (Varkon, max 20): active `+10% * level` attack.
  Window scales `15m` at L1 to `4h` at L20; cooldown after the window scales
  `24h` at L1 to `16h` at L20. Costs `200 * level` of each core resource.
- Eldritch Conduit (Veil, max 20): unlocks shard-based base upgrades
- Transmuting Void (Veil, max 20): convert mixed resources into one chosen
  resource at `1:1` total volume; duration 6h -> 15m

OUTPOST BUILDINGS
- Drill (outpost only, max 20): minute mining chance from 5% to 100%
- Refiner (outpost only, max 20): hourly SPU crafting and hourly Noctmark
  mint checks

DIRECTIVE PATHS
---------------
All 12 directive paths progress in parallel.
Each path has 10 stages.
Completing a path grants a permanent buff.

- INTEL / Signal Cartographer
  Buff: +12% spy survival

- RAIDER / Void Reaver
  Buff: +10% raid loot

- FIGHTER / Aegis Vanguard
  Buff: +8% fleet attack

- ECON / Solar Ledger
  Buff: +6% base production

- DIPLO / Concord Envoy
  Buff: +20% diplomacy penalty resistance

- TRADER / Starlane Broker
  Buff: +8% fleet cargo

- BIZ / Consortium Founder
  Buff: +8% base storage

- EXPAND / Frontier Ascendant
  Buff: +8% travel speed

- INDUSTRY / Forge Architect
  Buff: +8% build speed and +8% ship-build speed

- DEFENSE / Bastion Warden
  Buff: +10% base defense and +6% fleet defense

- LOGI / Flux Quartermaster
  Buff: +10% travel speed and +6% fleet cargo

- AUGMENT / Noctium Artificer
  Buff: +8% research speed and +10% transmute speed

QUICK REMINDERS
---------------
- Directive stages auto-complete. There is no claim action.
- Central Spire outpost slots unlock at L5, L10, L15, and L21-L25; each slot
  can be spent on a strategic, asteroid mining, or rare-metal mining outpost.
- Central Spire 20 unlocks one child colony from that origin.
- Shipyards are the highest-value tempo buildings for most war plans.
- Ion Shield, Nano Storage, and Astra safety multiply each other.
- Objective wins happen only when the map state can defend the Wormhole or
  Keystone plan.
